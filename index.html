Eagle Framing

Eagle Framing was established in 2001 and is based in Columbiana, AL. We provide complete contracting services to homeowners and rough framing packages to commercial and residential general contractors. Eagle Framing has a reputation for quality and unmatched attention to detail.

Eagle Construction Products

Eagle Construction Products provides wood structural components and rough hardware packages to general contractors throughout the Southeast. We have long-standing relationships with some of the premier contractors in the area. Competitive pricing and top notch service make ECP a valuable asset to your next wood framed project.
